Gulab jamun

Gulab jamun is an Indian dish. It is a delicacy served on special occassions or as a desert.

INGREDIENTS

  • Gulab Jamun Mix from the supermarket
  • sugar
  • water

METHOD OF PREPARATION

Gulab Jamun

  1. Put the mix in a bowl and add quarter glass of water
  2. Knead the mix into smooth dough
  3. Prepare small balls from the dough
  4. Put the small dough balls in a frying pan and fry them in medium heat
  5. Fry the balls until golden brown

Sugar Syrup

  1. Put a cup of sugar in a pan and add a quarter glass of water
  2. Heat them in a medium heat
  3. Add Cardamamon for scent
  4. Turn the heat off after the sugar has completely melted

Final touches

  1. Put the fried gulab jamun in a bowl and add the sugar syrup
  2. Put the bowl aside for sometime
  3. Enjoy your sugar soaked gulab jamun
